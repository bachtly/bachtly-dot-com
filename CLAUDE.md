# CLAUDE.md

Guidance for AI assistants working in this repo.

## Writing style (prose in pages, posts, meta text)

Write plainly, like a person. Strip out AI writing tells from any user-facing
text passage before finishing:

- **No em-dashes, en-dashes, or double/triple hyphens** (`—`, `–`, `--`, `---`)
  in prose. Use a period, comma, or parentheses instead. (Note: `---` frontmatter
  fences and Markdown `---` rules are fine; this rule is about sentences.)
- Avoid the "not only X but also Y" and "it's not just X, it's Y" constructions.
- Avoid formulaic triads and colon-then-list sentence padding.
- Avoid filler like "along the way", "in today's world", "at the end of the day",
  "delve", "seamless", "elevate", "unlock", "leverage" (as a verb), "robust".
- Keep the author's own voice and word choices; fix grammar, don't rewrite it into
  marketing copy.
